// Task 1: Making pbj's.
 
 // first step is making sure we have all the matriels needed to make a PBS.
 
 1. you need to have a slice of bread 
 2. you need to have peanut butter 
 3. you need to have jelly 
 4. you need to have a knife 

// second step is putting the peanut butter and jelly on the bread

1. take the knife and dip it in the peanut butter and spread it on top of the first slice of bread
2. then do the same thing for the jelly 
3. make sure you're spreading it evenly
4. take a second slice of bread and put it on top of the first one

// finally you have a pbj's sandwich
